CRISTIANO RONALDO

Cristiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveiro, born on February 5, 1985, in Funchal, Madeira, Portugal, is widely regarded as one of the greatest footballers of all time. Rising from humble beginnings, he joined Sporting CP's academy as a teenager before Manchester United signed him in 2003 for £12.24 million. Over the next two decades, he conquered every major league in Europe — the Premier League, La Liga, and Serie A — while winning five UEFA Champions League titles and five Ballon d'Or awards. His combination of explosive pace, aerial dominance, and an almost superhuman work ethic transformed him from a skillful winger into the most prolific goalscorer the sport has ever seen
